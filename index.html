Lunahuaná

Three hours from Lima, it has a beautiful landscape where the valley formed by the Cañete River stands out. Its location has turned this place into the perfect place for lovers of extreme sports, who can travel along the river on a raft or climb natural stone walls without having to go anywhere else. All in the same place.
